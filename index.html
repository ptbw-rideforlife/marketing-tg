What is Ride For Life?

Are you pregnant but have no way to the hospital to deliver your baby? We have the solution motorcycle ambulances you sign up on our application and can request an ambulance driver near you to pick you up.

Ride For Life As A Driver?

Do you enjoy being a motorcycle ambulance drive? Do you enjoy helping having a hand in the safety of a pregnant woman through her journey to the hospital to have her baby? We have the application for you where a woman can get ahold of the nearest driver to here and have a safe enjoyable ride to the hospital.
